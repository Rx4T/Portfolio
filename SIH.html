A Four-Pillar Solution

We propose an integrated system that leverages AI and geospatial technology to overcome these challenges, creating a seamless flow from data to decision-making.

AI-Based Asset Mapping

Using high-resolution satellite imagery and computer vision, we automatically identify and map key assets within FRA villages. This provides an unprecedented, data-rich view of the natural resources and infrastructure available to communities, forming the visual backbone of the FRA Atlas.

Decision Support System (DSS)

The DSS is the intelligence engine that connects data to action. It analyzes the mapped assets and socio-economic data to recommend the most suitable Central Sector Schemes (CSS) for each FRA holder or village, enabling precise and impactful development planning.
